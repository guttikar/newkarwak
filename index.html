A little about us

Founded in 1997 by Abhay Kardeguddi, an engineer, MBA (Marketing) and Hortuculturist. Karwak stands for Eco-friendly life. It is an initiative to offer Products and Services for environment friendly lifestyle. A concept to reduce carbon footprints - make earth greener and better place.
Karwak is a unique combination of beautiful design combined with recycled and environment friendly material. We at karwak believe in giving sustainable alternatives to replace plastic and non-degradable products. It is about spreading awareness on nature conservation and preservation by small changes in day to day life.

Made with love and 100% environment friendly material!
